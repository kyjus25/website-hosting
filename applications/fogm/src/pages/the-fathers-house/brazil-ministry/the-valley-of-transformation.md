# The Valley of Transformation
<iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/TVxUtJHsiFA" width="100%" height="400" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></iframe>

**There is an incredible project God is doing on the edge of Sao Paulo in a large valley**. This work is in a very poor village lacking hope and peace with many great needs known as Vila da Paz. This valley is filled with violence and a desperate people with no answers that has created an environment where drugs, violence and chaos run rampant. The people here live in darkness and fear reaching out for answers but are only finding emptiness and despair. God has spoken to us that there is only one hope for these people and that is our Lord Jesus Christ.

This project is starting to bring like-minded ministries together with one purpose and with the same mission under what God is doing there. We believe God is pouring out His Holy Spirit and has started a revival in a land and in a people that most won’t go near.

It is in this valley that Fire of God Ministries are being led by God to **unite ministries** and **working as one body** in order to complete what God has started. To fulfill this calling, we have started the preparations for building a church where we can also hold English classes using desks that have been donated by those having the same heart and passion for this mission.

We believe this English class will help many who would never step foot into a church and this ministry will help them to go do the calling God has placed on their lives but will also help bring countries together in oneness and into a common bond of Love. We believe<img src="/admin/uploads/donation%20of%20desks.jpg" class="alignleft" alt="Hearts of missions donationg to what God is already doing" /> this is only a start and a beginning. This bond of love will grow and reach to one another spilling into the streets transforming this valley and also flow into other places as well. The vision is helping all in need by bringing the Love of Jesus and Gods Kingdom by tearing down the walls of religion and bringing the body into one vessel that is united in one goal and purpose. Our prayer is that we will fulfill the scripture in bringing the body together in unity and maturity into the fullness into Jesus Christ. (Ephesians 4: 12-14)

As this project matures, we are planning a major outdoor event that will spread the fire of God to the whole valley. That fire can then <img src="/admin/uploads/vila%20da%20paz%20pic.jpg" class="alignright" alt="bringing the love of God and peace and hope of jesus to those in need" />burn and spread throughout all Brazil so hot that the boundaries of a country cannot contain it as many travel to other lands and other countries. The fire starts with a few but then quickly spreads into the streets and in more churches. We believe this transforming and purifying fire will change the hearts of man into a heart of passion for the lost creating a desire for missions to the nations. It is the time we live in that makes this is a possible reality. It is a world revival for the bride in white preparing for Jesus, the messiahs return and the marriage supper of the lamb. This revival can only happen as the body unites as one body and one mind under Jesus Christ bent on truth and repentance that is shared to others.
